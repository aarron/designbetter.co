Point of View (POV) Checklist 2

You can use also use this interactive form if you'd like to have your POV checklist emailed to you.

Your POV should:

  • __ Provide focus and frame the problem
  • __ Inspire your team
  • __ Provide a reference for evaluating competing ideas
  • __ Empower team members to make decisions in response to the high level goals of the team
  • __ Fuel brainstorms by suggesting “how might we” statements
  • __ Capture the hearts and minds of people you meet
  • __ Save you from the impossible task of developing solution concepts that are all things to all people
  • __ Allow you to revisit and reformulate the POV as you learn by doing
  • __ Guide your innovation efforts
